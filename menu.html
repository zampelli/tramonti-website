Other Locations

Bellagio is the join venture of the two most authentic and celebrated “local” Italian restaurants in Santa Cruz - Tramonti & Lago di Como - we are bringing the authenticity and the high quality of our chef’s experience in a casual and beach style “deli” concept. We proudly use mostly organic ingredients, local sourced or imported from Italy to prepare house made Gelato (yes, the real deal!), spectacular vegan sorbets, gourmet Panini and tramezzini (the “two bites” tea sandwiches), and espresso. #wecookthefoodwelove
