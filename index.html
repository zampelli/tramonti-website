Welcome

Tramonti serves authentic Italian pizza, pasta, fresh salads, regional specialties and house made desserts. We use locally-sourced, organic products or unique ones imported from Italy. We are in the heart of Seabright, steps from the beach. Our recipes reflect family traditions and the simplicity and warmth of a true Italian cusine.

About

Us

Tramonti is owned and operated by “first Italian” generation Luca & Matteo, along with their family and many Italians in the Santa Cruz community as part of our team. We bring the authentic lifestyle of the Bel Paese in all our dishes since 2012.
